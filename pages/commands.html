Commands

Find information about commands here!
Everything in between of these () brackets are required arguments, and everything in between of these [] brackets are optional arguments
Bot Economy Explore Help Shop

/invite

usage: "/invite"

Invite the bot into your own server.

/ping

usage: "/ping"

Shows you the latency of the bot.

/botinfo

usage: "/botinfo"

Shows you the statistics of the bot.

/vote

usage: "/vote"

Sends a link to the top.gg site to vote for out bot, so you can get a reward :)

/supporter

usage: "/supporter"

Let's you claim a bonus for being in our support server.

/gold

usage: "/gold [member:@user]"

Shows how much gold you have.

/stats

usage: "/stats [member:@user]"

Shows you your stats, that are stored in the database of the bot.

/booster

usage: "/booster"

Rewards the boosters of the support server with some gold.

/daily

usage: "/daily"

Gives you a daily reward.

/boss

usage: "/boss"

This command will allow you to fight a boss of the current area you are in.

/explore

usage: "/explore"

This command will allow you to explore the world.

/reset

usage: "/reset"

This command will reset your stats, but adds a multiplier to your gold and EXP gain.

/travel

usage: "/travel"

This command allows you to travel to a different area.

/leaderboard

usage: "/leaderboard (lb:gold, exp, resets, kills, spares, deaths)"

Displays the leaderboard for either the gold, exp, resets, kills, spares or deaths.

/event

usage: "/event"

This command will allow you to see the current/upcomming event.

/tutorial

usage: "/tutorial"

This command shows you how the bot works, what you can do, etc.

/help

usage: "/help"

This shows you all the bot's commands, and gives you a link to the website and support server.

/shop

usage: "/shop"

This command will allow you to buy stuff from the shop in that current area.

/crates

usage: "/crates"

Shows you your crates and lets you open them.

/inventory

usage: "/inventory"

This command will show your inventory.

/use

usage: "/use"

This command let's you use an item.
